How It Connects

For remote control, accounts should connect straight to the relay host from the autoexec. The dashboard can be hosted on Vercel, but the relay needs a WebSocket host such as Railway, Render, Fly.io, a VPS, or your home PC with a tunnel.

Local-only mode: set relay URL to ws://localhost:8787/admin and autoexec RELAY_HOST to localhost:8787.
